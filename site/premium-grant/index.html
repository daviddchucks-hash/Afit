👻 Ghost Mode

Hides this user from every search and user list in the app (admin panel, this tool, DB explorer) and wipes their email from the profile. They keep logging in and using their existing access exactly as before — nothing new is tracked about them going forward. Once ghosted, they can only be found again by their exact User ID.

⚠️ Danger Zone

Permanently deletes this user's profile, all of their exam results, and immediately invalidates their active login session. This cannot be undone.
